Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

GoSolarDaddy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our site or submit our quiz, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Home address or zip code
  • Monthly electric bill and utility provider
  • Homeownership status and roof type
  • Device type, browser, IP address, and general location (collected automatically)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you about solar options via phone, text, or email
  • Generate a solar savings estimate for your home
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. Communications

By submitting your information, you consent to receive calls, texts, and emails from GoSolarDaddy. We may use automated technology for these communications. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or contacting us at hello@gosolardaddy.com.

4. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely using Airtable, a cloud-based database platform. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

6. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@gosolardaddy.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

Questions about your data or this policy? Contact us at hello@gosolardaddy.com.
